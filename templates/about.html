JokeAPI

This site uses JokeAPI which is a REST API that delivers jokes.

It supports a variety of filters that can be applied to get just the right jokes.

Categorizes the jokes depending on what the joke is about or who the joke is directed at. A joke about programming will be in the "Programming" category, dark humor will be in the "Dark" category and so on.

Flags are a more fine layer of filtering. Multiple flags can be set on each joke and they tell you something about the offensiveness of each joke.

Each joke comes with one of two types: single or twopart.
